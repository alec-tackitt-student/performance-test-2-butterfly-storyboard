Butterflies

Home Facts Life Cycle About Me

Area: All contients except Antartica


Habitat: Everything from tropical forests to grasslands to tundra


Food: Caterpillars eat leaves; butterflies sip nectar, sap, and juices from fruit


Butterflies range in size from a tiny 1/8 inch to a huge almost twelve inches.


Female butterflies are usually larger than males and they even live longer.




Before a butterfly was ever a butterfly, it went through a few stages: Egg, Catapillars(larva), Chrysalis(pupa), Adult butterfly.
